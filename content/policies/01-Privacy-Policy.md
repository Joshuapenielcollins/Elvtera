## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways, depending on how you interact with us.

### 2.1 Personal Data

Information that identifies you as an individual, which may include your name, email address, phone number, job title, company name, billing address, and any details you share when you contact us or fill out a form.

### 2.2 Business Data

Information relating to your organisation and the projects we deliver for you. This can include company records, operational data, workflow configurations, customer databases you ask us to work with, CRM and ERP data, documents, and other material you provide so we can perform our services.

### 2.3 Usage Data

Technical information collected automatically when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type and version, device information, pages viewed, time spent on pages, referring URLs, and general interaction patterns.

### 2.4 Communication Data

Records of your correspondence with us across email, chat, WhatsApp, voice calls, support tickets, and meeting platforms, including the content of those communications and any attachments.

### 2.5 Payment Data

When you pay us, limited billing information is processed. We do not store full card numbers on our own systems. Payment details are handled directly by our payment processors, described in Section 5.

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties to run our business and deliver our services. These providers process information only as needed to perform their functions.

### 5.1 Payment Providers

Depending on your location and the contracting entity, payments may be processed through Bank Transfer, Stripe, Razorpay, Wise, PayPal, or UPI. Each provider handles your payment information under its own privacy terms.

### 5.2 AI Providers

Our services often involve artificial intelligence platforms including OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini. When a project requires it, data may be sent to these platforms to generate outputs, power AI agents, chatbots, voice agents, and automations. We take reasonable steps to limit what is shared and to select providers with appropriate data protection commitments. Where a provider offers options to exclude data from model training, we prefer those options for client work.

### 5.3 Infrastructure and Tooling Providers

We use cloud and software platforms such as AWS, Oracle Cloud, Azure, GCP, Supabase, Docker, n8n, ERPNext, Frappe, and Twenty CRM to host, build, and operate solutions. These providers may store or process data on our behalf.

### 5.4 Analytics and Communication Tools

We use analytics services and communication platforms, including WhatsApp for messaging automations, to run our website and interact with you.

A more detailed description of processors used in a specific engagement is available in our **Data Processing Addendum**.

On request, and subject to any legal holds, we will delete or return project data after an engagement ends, as set out in your contract and our Data Processing Addendum.
