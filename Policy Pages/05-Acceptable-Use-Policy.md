## 3. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use our website, services, or any solution we provide to do any of the following.

### 3.1 Spam

Sending unsolicited bulk messages, unauthorised marketing, or any communication that violates anti-spam laws, including through email, WhatsApp, SMS, or automated messaging systems.

### 3.2 Illegal Activity

Using our services for any unlawful purpose, or to promote, facilitate, or carry out any activity that breaks applicable laws or regulations.

### 3.3 Malware

Uploading, distributing, or transmitting viruses, worms, ransomware, spyware, or any other malicious code, or using our services to develop or spread such code.

### 3.4 Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineering, decompiling, disassembling, or attempting to derive the source code or underlying structure of our proprietary software, tools, or systems, except to the extent this restriction is prohibited by law.

### 3.5 Credential Sharing

Sharing login credentials, access keys, or accounts with unauthorised individuals, or allowing others to access services in a way that circumvents agreed usage terms or licensing.

### 3.6 Copyright Infringement

Uploading, using, or distributing content, data, or software that infringes the intellectual property rights of others, or instructing us to build solutions using material you do not have the rights to use.

### 3.7 AI Abuse

Using AI features, agents, chatbots, or voice systems we provide to generate illegal, harmful, deceptive, defamatory, or abusive content, to impersonate others without authorisation, to produce disinformation, or to circumvent the safety controls of AI providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini.

### 3.8 Fraud

Engaging in fraudulent activity, misrepresentation, phishing, or any scheme intended to deceive or defraud others through our services.

### 3.9 Security Attacks

Attempting to gain unauthorised access to systems, networks, or data, probing or scanning for vulnerabilities without permission, launching denial-of-service attacks, or otherwise interfering with the security or integrity of our infrastructure or that of third parties.

### 3.10 Bot Abuse

Deploying bots, scrapers, or automated processes in ways that violate the terms of the platforms involved, overload systems, or harvest data without authorisation.

### 3.11 Excessive API Usage

Exceeding agreed usage limits, making excessive or abusive API calls, or using integrations in a way that degrades performance for others or breaches the terms of underlying providers.
